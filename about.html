History

The OSSP was established in alignment with Commonwealth Act No. 502, which created Quezon City in 1939. This act, amended by Republic Act No. 537 and the Revised Charter of Quezon City, laid the groundwork for the city's governance structure. The OSSP's establishment is also related to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7160, known as the Local Government Code of 1991, which decentralized government functions and enhanced local autonomy.
